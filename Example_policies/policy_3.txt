Purpose of the processing of personal data
Customer relationship management and communication with potential customers.

Information content of the register
Name
Telephone number
E-mail
Other information that the user has entered on the form on the website.

Regular sources of information
Information provided by customers themselves.

Regular disclosures
The information will not be disclosed to third parties. The data will not be disclosed outside the EU or the EEA.

Registry security principles
All information in the register is stored in the management system and is protected and located in such a way that unauthorized parties do not have access to the information in the register.

Rights of the data subject
The person in the register has the right to request personal data about him or her from the controller and the right to request the rectification or deletion of such data.
